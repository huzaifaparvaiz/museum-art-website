Aurélie LaFleur

In "The Fragmented Self", Aurélie LaFleur's masterpiece, the artist's own face is shattered into a thousand pieces, like a mirror dropped on stone. Each shard reflects a different aspect of her being: the curve of a smile, the glint of a tear, the furrow of a brow. The fragments seem to hover in mid-air, suspended by invisible threads, as if the very fabric of identity is being rewoven before our eyes. It's a painting that defies the notion of a fixed self, instead embracing the fluid, fractured nature of human experience.

Éternité en Marbre

Golden Age classic sculpture, where artistry and philosophy converged in perfect harmony. Crafted with meticulous care, this piece embodies the ideals of eternal beauty and timeless grace that defined the era. The smooth contours and intricate details reflect the advanced techniques of Greek sculptors, who sought to capture the essence of the divine and the human form in their work.The marble, with its serene yet powerful presence, serves as a medium through which the sculptor’s vision of immortality and transcendence is realized. As a relic of a bygone golden age, “Éternité en Marbre” stands as a lasting tribute to the grandeur and enduring influence of ancient art.

“La peinture la plus célèbre.”

L'Inconnu Masqué

This evocative piece, rooted in the tradition of Greek artistry, presents a headless figure holding a mask—a powerful symbol of the duality between appearance and essence. The smooth, timeless marble captures the essence of ancient craftsmanship, while the absence of a head invites viewers to contemplate the nature of self and the roles we play. As the figure cradles the mask, it prompts an introspective journey into the concepts of anonymity and transformation, reflecting the rich philosophical inquiries of the Golden Age

Deux Âmes Séparées

Dans un monde où les chemins se croisent et se séparent, "Deux Âmes Séparées" est une exploration poignante de l’amour et de la perte. Cette œuvre magnifiquement sculptée capture l'essence de deux êtres dont les destins se sont éloignés, mais dont les liens restent éternels. Leurs figures, magnifiées par la pierre, sont à la fois proches et distantes, illustrant la complexité des émotions humaines. Chaque détail, minutieusement travaillé, symbolise les souvenirs partagés et les rêves brisés, offrant au spectateur une réflexion sur la nature de l’attachement et le passage du temps. Cette sculpture est un hommage à la beauté mélancolique des relations qui transcendent la séparation physique pour toucher l'âme."

Euphrosyne la Délicate/P>

This sculpture, inspired by the elegance of Greek artistry, depicts a woman whose form is adorned with intricate patterns and delicate textures that speak to the sculptor's exceptional skill. The meticulously carved features and flowing lines reflect the timeless beauty and sophisticated craftsmanship of the era. As "Euphrosyne" embodies joy and charm in Greek mythology, this piece pays homage to the harmonious blend of aesthetic refinement and profound artistic expression. It stands as a testament to the artist's dedication to creating a visual symphony that celebrates the divine elegance of the female form, resonating with the cultural and philosophical ideals of antiquity.
